Date Syrup

Premium Date Syrup/ Molasses is a natural and highly nutritious ingredient for numerous food and beverage industries. Our fully-automated date syrup/molasses production line guarantees a very high quality product.

Please Contact us for product specifications and specific inquires. We also carry 25kg buckets or 280kg drums and 1,400kg IBC tanks for wholesale clients.

WHY DATE SYRUP?

  • Delicious Natural Sweetener for beverages, energy bars, baby food and baked goods.
  • Enhanced flavoring for date milk, shakes, yogurts, ketchup, BBQ sauces, other molasses and syrups.
  • Healthy topping for pancakes, waffles, porridge and salads.
  • Healthy alternative to commercial honey and sugary syrup.
  • Cosmetic use in the form of face masks and hair masks.
